Insight Eight:

NS should explore how best to work with governments on the declaration of drought and food emergencies, promoting the use of objective tools and triggers, rather than political decisions.

  • Due to contingency plans and DM strengthening, the Kenya Red Cross started emergency response months before the national government declared the drought a national disaster.
  • Delays in drought emergency announcements (from Gov) led to support being provided after effects were being felt by communities. In Kenya, KRCS started responding to drought 4 months before the government declared the drought a national disaster. The Ethiopian government tried to respond to the drought but did not have the capacity, thus delaying humanitarian appeals and fund-raising initiatives.
